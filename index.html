Black and Hispanic adults are almost twice as likely as White adults to develop type 2 diabetes (T2D). Reasons for the disparities in care include structural barriers, racial discrimination, and distrust of the health care system. This live webinar with Q&A will discuss how clinicians can overcome these and other barriers to improve patient outcomes.
